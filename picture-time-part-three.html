Picture time! (Part Three)

james

Our big excursion as a couple was the safari.  From the brochure descriptions, these safaris sound totally cheesy and touristy -- and they are -- but it was also a lot of fun.  We didn't do everything that the excursion offered -- camel rides for instance -- but even so, we both had a very enjoyable time.  If I were to go back to Dubai, I'd probably do this again. [nggallery id=37]

Like the photos of the hotel, these photos of my wife around the pool are from different days during the week.  The pool was on the roof of the hotel, and I think we were up there a total of three times.  (Three beaches, three pool trips -- just about every day we were in some water.)
